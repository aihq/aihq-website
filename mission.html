Our Story

AIHQ aims to get people interested in Artificial Intelligence (AI) by showing them easy ways to get started. The website caters to people from all technical backgrounds in AI, all the way from complete novice to an expert. AIHQ is a portal to help people learn about AI, have access to tools and resources that increase their knowledge, connect them with professionals in the field, as well as help them use AI critically.
